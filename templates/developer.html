Meet Sudipta Mitra: Developer of the Health Diagnosis Web App

Bringing Innovation to Health Tech as a Student Developer

Hello! I’m Sudipta Mitra, a 3rd-year Data Science student at NSHM Knowledge Campus, Kolkata. I developed this AI-powered symptom-based disease diagnosis web application as a part of my journey to blend technology with healthcare and make medical insights more accessible to everyone.

My Learning and Passion for AI

With a strong academic foundation in computer science and a growing expertise in AI and Machine Learning, I have been exploring real-world applications of these technologies. This platform represents a hands-on project where I applied what I’ve learned to solve a practical problem—empowering users with quick and reliable health predictions.

Building with Purpose

From developing the backend using Flask to training the Machine Learning model and designing a clean user interface, I built every part of this platform with a focus on simplicity, accuracy, and usefulness. My goal was to create a tool that’s not only smart but also easy for anyone to use, regardless of their background.

AI Meets Healthcare

The creation of Medical Health Center reflects my belief that artificial intelligence can revolutionize healthcare by making it more proactive, personalized, and data-driven. This project is a step toward making intelligent healthcare tools available at your fingertips—especially helpful in remote or underserved areas.

Let’s Shape the Future Together

Thank you for visiting Medical Health Center. I hope this platform provides valuable health insights and inspires others to explore how technology can be used for the greater good. As a student developer, this is just the beginning—and I’m excited to keep learning, growing, and building solutions that matter.
